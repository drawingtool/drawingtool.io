About

Drawing is a universal language. This project aims to use drawing as a tool to provide a space for open discussion about topics people find difficult to verbalise*. Use this in tool workshops, discussion spaces, classrooms or the dinner table. Visualise your definition of a subject and compare with society's opinions of said topic.

This website accepted submissions for 3 months, constantly collecting the opinions
of users anonymously. The archive now hosts all user submissions and the content was collated and printed into a publication archiving today's opinions.
Created by Will Reuben and Noah Silver

*Topics sourced from UK Google trends data of common words searched to define. Back
