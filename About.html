About

Drawing is a universal language. This tool aims to provide a space for open discussion about topics we find difficult to verbalise*. Use this in workshops, discussion spaces, classrooms or the dinner table. Visualise your definition of a subject and compare with society's opinions of said topic.

This website will be live for 3 months, constantly collecting the opinions of our users anonymously. The content will be printed into a publication archiving today's opinions, with the digital archive updating weekly.

*The topics are sourced from UK Google trends data of words people searched to define. Back
